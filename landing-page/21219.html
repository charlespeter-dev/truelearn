Improving Classroom Preparedness and Engagement for Gen Z and Millennial Students: Leverage Technology to Drive Successful Course, Program, and Exam Outcomes

Using technology to effectively engage students of all life stages in the classroom can be challenging. The following webinar explores why differences exist in multigenerational learning and how to manage those learning differences with special emphasis on strategies that reach Generation Z and Millennial students.
