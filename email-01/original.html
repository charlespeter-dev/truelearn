How can educators better engage Generation Z and Millennial nursing students in the classroom to improve course, program, and NCLEX outcomes?
 
Picmonic is the answer. The leading audio-visual mnemonics platform offers animated lessons, virtual classrooms, and low-stakes quizzes that integrate flawlessly into the nursing curriculum – LMS integration is available too. Educators can assign Picmonics ahead of class to help students come prepared and surface insights into where knowledge gaps exist or utilize Picmonics to create active learning experiences in lectures keeping students engaged with an effective learning tool.

Find out how to cultivate successful learning environments in multi-generation classrooms and what educators can do to improve engagement and enhance teaching for effective learning.
